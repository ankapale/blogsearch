<h3 class='post-title'>
<a href='https://nlpers.blogspot.com/2009/04/naacl-program-up.html'>NAACL program up</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
<div class='post-body'>
<p>See <a href="http://clear.colorado.edu/NAACLHLT2009/program.html">here</a>.  I see a bunch that I reviewed and really liked, so I'm overall pretty happy.  (Though I also see one or two in the "other" category :P.)<br /><br />At any rate, here are the top stemmed/stopped words with frequencies:<br /><ul><li>model (30)<br /></li><li>base (19)<br /></li><li>translat (17)<br /></li><li>languag (16)<br /></li><li>speech (14)<br /></li><li>improv (14)<br /></li><li>statist (12)<br /></li><li>machin (12)<br /></li><li>unsupervis (11)<br /></li><li>recognit (10)<br /></li><li>pars (10)<br /></li><li>system (9)<br /></li><li>learn (9)<br /></li><li>word (8)<br /></li><li>depend (8)<br /></li><li>approach (8)<br /></li><li>spoken (7)<br /></li><li>semant (7)<br /></li><li>search (7)<br /></li><li>linear (7)<br /></li><li>extract (7)<br /></li></ul>Looks like machine translation and unsupervised learning are the winners.</p>
