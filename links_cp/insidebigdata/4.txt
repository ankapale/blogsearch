IFS 
 IFS is a public company (OMX STO: IFS) founded in 1983 that develops, supplies, and implements IFS ApplicationsT, a component-based extended ERP suite built on SOA technology. IFS focuses on agile businesses where any of four core processes are strategic: Service & asset management, manufacturing, supply chain and projects. The company has 2,000 customers and is present in more than 50 countries with 2,700 employees in total.
